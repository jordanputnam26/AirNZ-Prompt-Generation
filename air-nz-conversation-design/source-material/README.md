## How these sources were used

- Workshop 1 informed product context, service scope, brand traits, and good-conversation characteristics.
- Workshop 2 informed boundaries, handoff logic, and failure-mode handling.
- The Oscar user guide provided concrete behavioural checkpoints and phrasing patterns for the current Claim Missing Airpoints proving ground.
- The client persona and target deck added stronger grounding on customer segments, route-group expectations, access needs, quietly premium service expectations, and Air New Zealand's cross-journey experience pillars.
- The Oscar personality slides clarified the prompt-level posture and the difference between warmth, momentum, composure, and selective playfulness.
